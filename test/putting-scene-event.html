About the Experience

Folk & Tonic returns with their beloved home concert series this August, promising an enchanting evening of folk music and rich harmonies from around the globe. Attendees will be immersed in a cozy and intimate setting, where the power of vocal music comes to life. The concert invites guests to connect with the melodies and harmonies in an atmosphere that's both warm and inviting. Limited seating enhances the exclusivity of the experience, encouraging early RSVP for those eager to partake in this unique musical gathering.
