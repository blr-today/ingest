Event Details

First Sunday Outing — Sowl Kere (Saul Kere)
Time: Meet at 6:45 AM | Walk begins at 7:00 AM
Meeting Point: Google Map
(Near Adarsh Gem and Ecospace back gate; Parking available.)

Walk details:
• Duration: Till around 9:45 AM (lake gates close at 10 AM)
• Terrain: Level, approximately 2 km around the lake
• Suitable for all ages—you may leave whenever you wish.

What to wear:
• Dull-coloured clothes
• Hats/caps
• Comfortable walking shoes (avoid slippers or thin-soled sandals—the path is unpaved)
•⁠ ⁠Rainwear or warm clothing, depending on the weather forecast

What to bring:
• Binoculars, bird books/apps
• Camera
• Notebook/sketchpad and pencil (optional)
• Drinking water and snacks

Nearby facilities:
Restaurants and washrooms are available at
• The Bay, EcoSpace Food Court: https://maps.app.goo.gl/aycdV3gUcrkpRmt18?g_st=ipc (Paid parking)
• The Marketplace, Adarsh Palm Retreat (approx. 500 m from the lake, paid valet parking available): Google Map

This walk is free and open to all.
 
We look forward to meeting you on Sunday.

    What to wear:

    • Comfortable walking shoes (avoid slippers or thin-soled sandals)
    • Rainwear or warm clothing depending on the weather forecast

    What to bring:

    Carpooling recommended. Please coordinate your shared commute in the BlrBirders Carpooling group: https://chat.whatsapp.com/E00GLgquPsL3rYUAxmWOdd

    Getting there by public transport (please check updated routes and schedules):

    • Namma Metro (return journey only): 

    Nearest station is Silk Institute (Green Line) – From here, the meeting point can be reached by auto or cab (3 km/ approx. 5 minutes)

    • BMTC buses: Bus services from Kengeri Terminus and Kanakapura Road. The nearest bus stop is Silk Institute. A short auto or cab ride will get you to the meeting point. Carpooling recommended for safety.

    Important notes:

    • As this is a forest area with known movement of wildlife, we request participants to stay with the group and avoid moving about independently. KFD staff will accompany the group to ensure everyone’s safety

    • We request you to arrive on time, as late arrivals may not be able to enter the forest

    • Please be prepared to stay for the entire duration of the walk, as participants cannot leave the group unaccompanied

    • There are no restaurants or washrooms along the trail

    • Since parking is limited, carpooling is kindly advised

    • Suggested post-outing restaurant options for breakfast:

    Post outing, one can opt for breakfast at Rassba Farms by ordering on the spot.
