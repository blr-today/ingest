Event Details

Malhar Laugh Club brings you one of Bangalore's funniest comics to Koota. Catch Ashwin Srinivas Live on 24th May, 2024.

After his take on Open Relationship and Team Lunch gains millions of views across YouTube and Instagram, Ashwin is back in Bangalore to keep doing what he does. Get your friends and enjoy the night of Standup comedy. Ashwin is an introvert who slogged in the IT world and lives in the comic world. His comedy revolves around himself and his reality. His comedy is a counter-punch to the world who considers him uncool.

Ashwin will be hosted by our very own Sandeep Nanu - birder, rescuer, trickster and comic.
