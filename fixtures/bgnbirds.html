Event Details

Where: Saul Kere

Event Details

WhereHesaraghatta Grasslands, we meet at the Animal Quarantine And Certification Services Hessarghatta Office and proceed from there towards ‘Rassba Farm’, where we will park

Event Details

Where
Hesaraghatta Grasslands, we meet at the Animal Quarantine And Certification Services Hessarghatta Office and proceed from there towards ‘Rassba Farm’, where we will park our vehicles and use their facilities. We cross over to the grasslands from there to start the walk.

Nearest Bus Stop: CFSP Farm Stop, 2.6 Km/30 mins walk to the animal quarantine center office. Buses plying from Yelahanka towards Byatha stop here.

Map
We meet at https://maps.app.goo.gl/hS7DyGVL2FMJcnGh7 (please follow Google Maps to this place, to avoid muddy roads as much as possible) and proceed towards https://maps.app.goo.gl/5oUqJRzyANaJ735J7.

Wear: Dull-coloured clothes, hats/caps, comfortable walking shoes, rain gear if there is rain forecast.

Bring: Notebook and a pen/pencil, and water.

Optional: Bird books/apps, binoculars, cameras, and any snacks you may need. 

Post outing, one can opt for breakfast at Rassba Farms by ordering on the spot.

We look forward to meeting you on Sunday.
