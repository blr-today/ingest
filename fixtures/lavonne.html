Weekend Calendar

All day courses every Saturday

Lavonne’s popular short-term courses are held every weekend. We offer a wide range of classes ranging from bread and chocolate making to French pastries and varieties of gateau. Typically suited for those aiming to pursue a newfound interest in baking and confectionery or those that require fine tuning of already acquired pastry making skills, our weekend classes pack quite a punch! These hands-on classes are also combined with a fair amount of theoretical learning. And of course, students get to take home their baked creations at the end of the class. Regularity is the key to mastery, with the Weekend Course Package you can attend 8 of your favourite classes for a flat ₹35,000.
