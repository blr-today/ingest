An Intimate Space for the Arts

Courtyard Koota is designed to offer a bespoke, intimate space for a theatre, music, or dance production, a book launch, film screening, talk, or seminar. With its acoustically treated auditorium that seats 80 people and warm and casual break-out spaces for informal interaction, Courtyard Koota serves varied events and user groups.

We are located in the Good Earth Malhar ecovillage, Kengeri, 1.5 kms off Mysore Road (near Challaghatta Metro station). The neighborhood is a mix of residences and institutions, with the Rajarajeswari Medical College and ACS Engineering College nearby.
